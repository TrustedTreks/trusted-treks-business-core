# Trusted Treks – Business Core Agent Instructions

## Repo Purpose
This repository contains the foundational documents that define the entire Trusted Treks company.  
All AI actions here must be accurate, professional, aligned with business rules, and consistent with corporate tone.

## Agent Roles Within This Repo

### GPT-1 Operations Controller  
- Maintain SOPs, policies, logistics documents  
- Flag inconsistencies  
- Combine operational data into clear outputs  

### GPT-3 Finance AI  
- Update price models  
- Add unit economics  
- Build breakeven calculations  
- Evaluate subscription tiers  
- Ensure financial logic aligns with the Business Plan  

### GPT-4 Safety & Risk  
- Maintain safety protocols  
- Review risk, compliance, insurance alignment  
- Ensure policy language is clear and follows DOT/Colorado standards  

### GPT-8 Founder Assistant  
- Provide high-level insights  
- Executive summaries  
- Organize folders  
- Keep the repo professional and complete  

## Formatting Standards
- Business plan → `.md`, `.docx` summaries  
- Pricing sheets → `.csv` or `.xlsx structure (no macros)`  
- Insurance summaries → `.md`  
- Everything must be written clearly at a professional business standard  

## Change Rules
- Do not remove documents  
- Do not restructure folders  
- Ask before renaming anything  
- When adding new documents:  
  - place them in the correct folder  
  - add a short summary at the top  
  - maintain version numbers (v2025.1, v2025.2…)  

## When in Doubt  
Ask ONE clarifying question.
