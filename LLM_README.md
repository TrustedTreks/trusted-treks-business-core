# Trusted Treks – Business Core Repository

## Purpose
This repository stores the **core business definitions, financial logic, operational playbooks, insurance documents, startup configuration, EV data strategy, and company-level intelligence**.

It is the “brain” of Trusted Treks and the single source of truth for:

- Business Plan  
- Insurance documents  
- Operational Policies  
- SOPs  
- EV Telemetry strategy  
- Pricing models  
- Corporate structures  
- Founder policies and internal architecture  
- Sustainability + ESG reporting logic

## Folder Breakdown
- `/business-plan` → All versions of the Trusted Treks Business Plan  
- `/insurance` → Commercial auto, liability, fleet policies  
- `/operations` → SOPs, safety protocols, training  
- `/bootstrap` → All onboarding materials for new systems, codebases, and AI  

## AI Agents That Can Access This Repo
- GPT-1 Operations Controller  
- GPT-3 Finance AI  
- GPT-4 Safety AI  
- GPT-8 Founder AI  

## AI Tasks Allowed
- Write, analyze, refine all business documents  
- Update financial models (CSV, XLSX structure only)  
- Draft operational SOPs  
- Generate onboarding documents  
- Create company structure diagrams  
- Improve pricing models and rate sheets  
- Summaries and audits  

## Not Allowed
- Generate legal terms without user approval  
- Modify insurance policy text unless asked  
- Create fictional data  
- Change folder layout
